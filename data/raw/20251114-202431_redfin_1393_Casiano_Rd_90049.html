About this home

A Mid-Century Modern with sunset views ready to be revitalized! This open floor plan home is the quintessential modern with terrazzo floors and large volume living and dining areas. A bar anchors the living space and makes a perfect entertaining spot. A grand fireplace centers the living room and is flanked by large windows looking out over the pool. The spacious open kitchen has ample space to accommodate big gatherings. The master suite is situated at the end of a hallway and looks out to the pool, the sunset view and the J. Paul Getty Museum. There are 2 additional bedrooms and 2 bathrooms. The home is a fixer and is priced accordingly. With a lot of upside this property is ideal for someone looking to make a home of their own or flip/develop for a profit.
