About this home

Located in Lower Hancock Park's historic Oxford Square, 1120 S Lucerne Blvd. presents a unique improvement opportunity. This charming home offers 2 bedrooms and 1 bathroom across just over 1,000 square feet of living space, set on a generous lot of nearly 7,200 square feet. Just blocks from Harold Henry Park and Queen Anne Park, the property is ideally positioned with convenient access to the 10 Freeway. Enjoy proximity to popular restaurants, supermarkets, and entertainment venues, all within a lively and welcoming neighborhood community.
